Lookbook 2022/2023

KAIFUKU is a exclusive fashion brand in Indonesia. The focus of the brand are local batik pattern made out of high-quality materials like high-quality cotton. All shirt, blouse, and dress are made with high attention to detail.

Delivery is an important part as well. Ordered shirt, blouse, and dress are delivered personally in most of the cities in Indonesia and to show gratitude, each product is packed with our signature box.

The brand started as an idea when I reconnected with my friend during the COVID-19 times.

After spending more time with her, I noticed that she has the most amazing personality I have ever known. She has a lot of potential for a great digital business. I tried to connect the knowledge she had with my skills in design and my humble business skills. I will never meet someone great like her again
